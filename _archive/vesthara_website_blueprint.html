The Strategy

Story + trust + product — in that order

1

Hook — stop the scroll in 3 seconds

A visitor decides to stay or leave in 3 seconds. Your hero section must answer: "What is this? Is it for me? Can I trust it?" — all instantly.

"Six yards of Kerala. Woven for you." — above the fold, full screen, one powerful image.

3 second rule
2

Story — make them feel something

Before you sell, make them connect. A short 3-line brand story below the hero builds emotional attachment that no product photo can.

"Every Vesthara saree begins on a handloom in Kerala. Made by weavers who have spent a lifetime with their craft. Worn by women who carry that story with pride."

emotional pull
3

Trust — prove you are real

Before they add to cart, customers need to know you're real. Social proof, reviews, guarantees, and WhatsApp access do this job.

Show real customer faces, real WhatsApp screenshots, real weaver photos — not stock images.

social proof
4

Product — now they are ready to buy

After story and trust, present your products. They now care about what you sell because they care about who you are.

High-quality photos, detailed descriptions, clear pricing, easy add to cart — frictionless buying.

conversion
5

Remove friction — make buying effortless

Every extra click loses a customer. WhatsApp button always visible, one-click checkout, COD option, clear return policy — zero hesitation.

"Order on WhatsApp in 60 seconds" — the safety net that converts hesitant buyers.

zero friction

Headless Tech Stack

What to build with, and why

Shopify (backend)

Products, inventory, orders, payments, Razorpay integration, Shiprocket shipping. Never touch this — it just works.

Next.js (frontend)

React framework for your headless storefront. Blazing fast, SEO-perfect, customisable to any design. Free to deploy on Vercel.

Shopify Storefront API

Connects your Next.js frontend to Shopify backend. Products, collections, cart, checkout — all via API. GraphQL.

Vercel (hosting)

Deploy your Next.js site free. Global CDN — your site loads fast in Delhi, Mumbai, and Bangalore equally.

Free Shopify headless starter — use this

Next.js Commerce (Vercel)

Free open-source headless starter by Vercel built specifically for Shopify. Go to github.com/vercel/commerce — clone, connect your Shopify store, deploy in 1 hour.

Hydrogen (Shopify)

Shopify's own headless React framework. Free, officially supported, best performance. Start at shopify.dev/custom-storefronts/hydrogen.

Key Shopify apps to install

Superlemon (WhatsApp) · Judge.me (reviews) · Klaviyo (email) · Privy (popups) · Shiprocket (shipping) · Razorpay (payments). All free to start.

Performance target

Headless gives you 90+ Google PageSpeed score. Aim for under 2 second load time on mobile 4G in India. Speed = conversion = rankings.
